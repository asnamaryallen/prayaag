About Codex

Codex is the Computer Science and Information Technology Department Association at LBS Institute of Technology for Women, dedicated to fostering technical excellence, innovation, and professional growth among aspiring technologists. We bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world applications through coding competitions, workshops, and tech talks, with PRAYAAG as our flagship tech fest. From software development challenges to emerging technology initiatives, Codex empowers students to become skilled problem-solvers and future leaders in the digital world.
