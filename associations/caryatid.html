About Caryatid

Caryatid is the Civil Engineering Department Association at LBS Institute of Technology for Women, committed to fostering technical excellence, innovation, and professional growth among aspiring civil engineers. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications through hands-on projects, competitions, and workshops at PRAYAAG, our premier tech fest. From structural design challenges to sustainable construction initiatives, Caryatid empowers students to become the architects of tomorrow's built environment.
